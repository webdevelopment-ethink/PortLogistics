Purpose-Built Facility in the Port Estate

Strategically located within the Port of Brisbane for optimal container freight operations.

Designed for Port Operations

Our depot at 70 Lucinda Drive is purpose-built for container freight station operations, located within the Port of Brisbane estate for direct access to all major container terminals.

Being positioned inside the port precinct means your cargo benefits from minimal transport distances, faster turnaround times, and seamless integration with terminal operations. Our facility meets all modern logistics requirements while maintaining the agility to handle diverse cargo types.

Facility Features

  • Undercover & Container-Rated Hardstand

    Multiple hardstand areas suitable for laden containers, with undercover options for weather-sensitive cargo.

  • Truck-Friendly Access & Turning

    Wide gates, ample manoeuvring space, and efficient traffic flow designed for B-double and rigid truck access.

  • Secure Depot with 24/7 Monitoring

    Fully fenced compound with CCTV surveillance and controlled access for maximum cargo security.

  • Proximity to Container Terminals

    Walking distance to Brisbane's major container terminals for immediate pickup and delivery coordination.

  • Professional Equipment Fleet

    Modern forklifts, reach stackers, and handling equipment operated by experienced personnel.

  • Biosecurity-Compliant Facilities

    AQIS-approved wash bays and inspection areas for quarantine compliance.

Driver & Visitor Information

Important information for drivers and contractors accessing our facility

Site Access Requirements

  • Valid driver's licence and vehicle registration
  • Port of Brisbane access pass (MSIC card for regular visitors)
  • Safety induction completion (first-time visitors)
  • Booking confirmation or delivery documentation
  • PPE: High-vis vest, steel-cap boots (minimum)

On-Site Safety Rules

  • Follow all directional signage and traffic management
  • Report to reception immediately upon arrival
  • Remain in designated safe zones while on foot
  • No mobile phone use while driving on site
  • Speed limit: 10km/h throughout facility
